## COMPANY

- Name: Al-Aqsa Security & Logistics Services Ltd.
- Type: Private company (NOT government / port authority job)
- Services: Security, escort, cargo supervision for imported goods
- Cargo types: Dal, wheat, corn, soybean, poultry feed, animal feed
- Operation area: Chittagong port and domestic waterway routes

## JOB POSITION: Survey Scout / Escort Guard

### Duties
- Ship cargo supervision during load/unload
- Tally counting (Tallyman/Scaleman)
- Safeguard goods from theft/damage/loss
- Monitor transportation from ship to destination
- Report issues to operations team

### Qualifications
- Age: 18-45
- Education: NOT mandatory. Honesty, fitness, responsibility matter more.
- Higher education = better salary, office career path
- Experience: NOT required. Newcomers accepted.
- Physical fitness required
- NID or birth registration required

### Training
- Minimum 45-day training for newcomers
- Training salary: 12,000 - 15,000 BDT (salary + allowance)
- Covers: risk assessment, surveillance, emergency response, fire safety, customer service

### Salary
- Training period: 10,000 - 15,000 BDT/month
- After training: 12,000 - 18,000+ BDT/month
- Depends on: duty type, shift, location, experience
- Benefits: overtime, special duty allowance, advance/loan facility, Eid bonus
- Payment: Monthly. Processed by 10th of next month.
- If joined after 20th: salary starts from 3rd month
- Condition: Max 2 days absent during 1st-10th of month

### Joining Fee
- Amount: 3,500 BDT (one-time)
- Installment: 500 BDT/month deduction if not paid upfront
- Minimum at joining: 1,500 BDT
- Refund: After 6 months
- Covers: meals setup, ID card, uniform, ship boarding permit
- NO bribe, NO deposit beyond this

### Accommodation
- FREE accommodation provided
- On ship: sleep on ship during duty (5-15 days continuous)
- Off duty: office accommodation in Chittagong
- Why free: workers come from distant districts

### Food
- Self-expense through mess system
- Advance: 1,000-2,000 BDT to join mess
- Everyone shares mess costs proportionally
- Why not free: individual taste/quantity/timing varies

### Ship / Travel
- Domestic routes ONLY. No abroad travel.
- Routes: Narayanganj, Bhairab, Ashuganj, Pabna, Jessore, Mongla, Barisal
- Stay: 5-15 days continuous on ship per assignment
- Cannot commute from home during duty
- Duty ends → duty slip signed → photo sent via WhatsApp → payment via bKash/Nagad in 2-3 hours

### Required Documents
- Education certificate (if available)
- Chairman/Commissioner certificate
- Parents' voter ID (NID) photocopy
- 2x passport-size photos
- 2x stamp-size photos
- If no education cert: parents' NID as substitute

### Required Items to Bring
- Clothes (5-15 days worth)
- Mosquito net (মশারি)
- Blanket/quilt (কাঁথা)
- Bed sheet (চাদর)
- Towel (গামছা)

### Leave Policy
- First 3 months: NO leave
- Salary determined by submitted duty slips
- After probation: leave as per company rules

### Resignation
- 30-day written notice required
- Without notice: 1 month salary equivalent penalty
- Do not leave without informing office

### Office Location
- Address: Ispahani Container Depot Gate 1, Khokon's Building (3rd Floor), AK Khan Mor, Pahartali, Chittagong
- Visit hours: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Come prepared with all documents + belongings for same-day joining

### Career Growth / Future
- 6+ months experience = valuable credential
- Opens: shipping, logistics, warehouse, supervision, office sector
- Higher education holders: faster office career path
- Many past workers have moved to better companies

## TRUST / SCAM HANDLING
